## Overview

Contemporary Learning is a powerful SaaS platform designed for educators and organizations to create, manage, and deliver interactive educational content. You can build multi-page courses, design engaging assessments like MCQs and true/false questions, and organize documentation spaces efficiently. The platform emphasizes transformative pedagogy, shifting from traditional didactic methods to technology-mediated, authentic student learning experiences.

With tools for uploading HTML presentations, Keynote zips, and rich text editing (bold, italic, lists, alignments), you streamline content creation. Access your dashboard at `https://app.contemporarylearninghub.co.za` to get started.

## Benefits of Transformative Pedagogy

Transformative pedagogy empowers you to move beyond transmitting information to fostering deep, authentic student learning. Contemporary Learning mediates this through intuitive tools:

- **Didactic to Dynamic**: Shift from static teaching to interactive experiences.
- **Technology Integration**: Embed videos, images, and custom components effortlessly.
- **Scalable Documentation**: Manage project docs alongside courses in one space.

## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up and Login" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.contemporarylearninghub.co.za` and use OTP login. Enter your email to receive the code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Course" icon="plus">
    Click "Create" and select "Multi-page Course". Add components like text editors or question builders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Content" icon="edit-3">
    Format with bold, lists, or alignments. Upload presentations via drag-and-drop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and Share" icon="share-2">
    Preview with "Continue", then publish. Share links with learners.
  </Step>
</Steps>
