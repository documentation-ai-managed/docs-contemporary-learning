## Steps to Begin

1. Sign up at `https://app.contemporarylearning.com`
2. Confirm your email address
3. Log in with your credentials
4. Create or join a project workspace
5. Start adding and organizing documentation pages

## Quick Tip

Your workspace lets you invite team members and manage permissions easily from the dashboard.
