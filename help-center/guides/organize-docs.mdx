## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new doc">
    ````
    Click the "+ New Page" button in your workspace.
    Name your page and select a template if desired.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange pages">
    ````
    Drag and drop pages in the sidebar to reorder or nest them.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share with team">
    ````
    Use the "Share" option to invite collaborators or set permissions.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
