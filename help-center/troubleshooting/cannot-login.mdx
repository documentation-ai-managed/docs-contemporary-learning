---
title: Cannot Log In
description: Fix login issues on Contemporary Learning.
---

## Symptoms
- Error: "Invalid email or password"
- Login page reloads repeatedly

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    ````
    Go to the login screen and click "Forgot password?".
    Follow emailed instructions to reset.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Clear cookies and cached data, then try again.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
