## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A valid email address for OTP verification
- Internet connection for dashboard access
</Callout>

Contemporary Learning provides a simple web-based interface. No downloads required.

## Register and Log In with OTP

Follow these steps to create your account and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Login Page" icon="login">
    Navigate to `https://app.contemporarylearninghub.co.za` (or your Contemporary Learning dashboard URL).

    Click **Create Account** or **Sign Up** if you don't have an account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Email" icon="mail">
    Provide your email address.

    Click **Send OTP** to receive a one-time password via email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify with OTP" icon="shield-check">
    Enter the 6-digit code from your email.

    Click **Login** or **Continue**. You now access the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your email securely. Use the same browser for consistent sessions.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Multi-Page Course

Build a course in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start New Course" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Course** or **Multi-page Course**.

    Enter a title like "Introduction to Learning Design".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Pages" icon="file-plus">
    Click **Continue** to add pages.

    Use the editor for didactic content or transformative pedagogy sections.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish" icon="rocket">
    Review pages, then click **Create** or **Done**.

    Share the course link with students.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Text and Basic Formatting

Enhance content with the built-in editor.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Text Formatting" icon="type">
    Select text and use toolbar icons:

    - **Bold** (`_format_bold_`), **Italic** (`_format_italic_`)
    - Lists: numbered (`_format_list_numbered_`), bulleted (`_format_list_bulleted_`)
    - Headings: H1, H2, H3

    Add links: highlight text, click `_link_`, enter external address.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Alignment and Colors" icon="align-left">
    - Align: left (`_format_align_left_`), center (`_format_align_center_`), right, justify
    - Insert images: click `_image_` icon
    - Colors: Access palette for custom shades like brand color `#BB2649`

    For advanced: upload HTML or Keynote ZIP files.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Interactive Components" icon="interactive">
    Add MCQ: Use **MCQ Question Editor**, set options, click **Continue**.

    True/False: **True False Editor**, add statements.

    Indent: left/right indent icons for structured content.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
